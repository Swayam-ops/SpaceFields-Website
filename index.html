TESTIMONIALS

Shri Narendra Modi, Hon'ble Prime Minister of India

“Our space program has been possible due to innumerable young scientists of the country. We take pride in the fact that the satellites developed by our students and Sounding Rockets have reached space. The Sounding Rockets made by Odisha university engineers have also created many records. Our Space Program provides an impetus to children to think big and reach across those boundaries, which were considered impossible till today.”

Dr. K Sivan, Chairman ISRO & Secretary, Dept of Space

“It is my pleasure to note the high level of enthusiasm of the engineers of VSSUT towards space science and technology. The design and realization of the propulsion system, structure of the sounding rocket and payload is remarkable. To carry out an impossible task is called ‘Rocket Science’ and such activity has been performed by these young technocrats. I must compliment them for their excellent aptitude to take science & technology of the country to a higher level.”

Dr. Jitendra Singh, Hon'ble Union Minister of Space, (MoS) Science & Technology

“Extremely delighted to meet a group of engineering trainees who have developed the first-ever Satellite Launch Vehicle purely through indigenous effort. India is a frontline nation in the field of space technology. The efforts of these young engineers reflect their talent, curiosity and capabilities. First time a Space Launch Vehicle (SLV) has been developed by the students, which will inspire other students to excel in science and technology.”

Shri Dharmendra Pradhan, Union Minister of Education, Skill & Entrepreneurship

“I congratulate the team of young engineers for their outstanding research in the field of rocket launching. Through their unprecedented superlative endeavour, these gifted technocrats have set records by successfully launching rockets using indigenous knowhow. Under the leadership of such youth, the development of the nation will be accelerated. It is a matter of pride for Odisha that ISRO has chosen VSSUT as the exclusive incubation centre.”

Shri Naveen Patnaik, Hon'ble Chief Minister of Odisha

“I am proud that young innovators and budding space scientists from Odisha have developed a reusable sounding rocket with a Picosatellite. They have received worldwide acclaim for their achievement. These incredible youngsters exhibit the abundant potential of youth in innovation and inventions, more importantly with a keen desire to find solutions to major problems faced by mankind through use of science and technology.”

IAS Sanjay Singh, Commissioner-cum-Secretary of Skill Development & Technical Education, Govt of Odisha

“I must congratulate these young scientists who are instrumental in forging this coveted partnership with ISRO, through their talent, perseverance and glittering achievement in the field of Space research and Innovation. It is highly praiseworthy that students have developed a culture in rocket science and have proved their mettle by exemplary display of skills and talents at both national and international levels.”

Prof. (Dr.) Debadutta Mishra, PIC VSSSIC & Director TBI (VSSUT - ASSIST)

“The team is highly dedicated and enthusiastic. Their unprecedented achievements are trendsetters for thousands of students. We, as faculty, are extending all the support and are encouraging their pursuit. Through the MoU, ISRO is committed to offer technical consultancy as well as access to launching pads, test facilities, space laboratories. We all are working hand in hand to forge an environment for promoting Innovation & Entrepreneurship in the region.”
