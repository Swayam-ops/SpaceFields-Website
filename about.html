ABOUT US

We are a team of like-minded hustlers, engineers and space aficionados who believe in the innate intangible desire to explore the material universe and pursue the risk of venturing beyond our terrestrial home.

SpaceFields offers integrated suite of products and services, to minimize the barriers to entry, for emerging Small Satellites as well as humankind in general, to access and utilize space. We build next generation rockets to stimulate commercialization, democratization and exploration of space.
