LAUNCH VEHICLES

Our series of dedicated Small-Lift Orbital Launch vehicles namely - Garuda, Pawanhans, Airavata.

In the Hindu mythology, Garuda (the divine steed of Lord Vishnu), Pawanhans (the divine vehicle of Goddess Saraswati & Lord Brahma), and Airavata (The Elephant of the clouds & Warrior mount of Lord Indra) are celestial ‘vahanas’ emblematic of virility, celerity, ingenuity and thrust. They are not mere launch vehicles to ferry satellites but cosmic shuttles to usher humankind into a new era of spaceflight.
