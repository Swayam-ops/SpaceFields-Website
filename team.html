Advisors

Tribikram Mohapatra
Ex-GM (Aerospace), HAL Bangalore

Tribikram Mohapatra

Tribikram Mohapatra is a seasoned aerospace professional having 40 years of distinguished experience in the manufacturing of Space Launch Vehicles, Missiles, Military Aircrafts, UAVs and Satellites. As General Manager (Aerospace & Aviation) he has immensely contributed in bluechip projects such as manufacturing of PSLV, GSLV, INSAT and IRS satellites of ISRO and Prithvi Missiles for DRDO at HAL, Indigenous manufacturing of Hawk AJT, Jaguar Strike and Trainer Aircraft projects. As the Advisor-Skill development in HAL, he was instrumental in initiatives like formation of the Aerospace and Aviation Sector Skill Council, Adoption of Industrial Training Institutes under CSR, setting up Centre of Excellence in Advanced Manufacturing, Skill Development Centre setup jointly by HAL and IISc.
